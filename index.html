FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Tickets will be available through our official ticketing partners once show dates are announced. Sign up for our mailing list to get presale access and be notified when tickets go on sale.

Most of our shows are all ages, but check for each show before buying tickets. Specific age requirements will be listed on each event page. Minors may be required to be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

We host shows at various venues throughout Louisville, KY. Venue information, including address, parking details, and accessibility info, will be provided once show dates are confirmed.

All sales are final unless an event is cancelled. If a show is cancelled, refunds will be issued automatically to the original payment method. For postponed shows, tickets remain valid for the new date.

Small personal cameras and phones are generally allowed for personal use. Professional cameras with detachable lenses and video recording equipment are typically not permitted without prior approval. Check specific show details for restrictions.

Parking availability varies by venue. We'll provide detailed parking information, including nearby lots and street parking options, on each show's event page. We encourage carpooling or using rideshare services.

Yes! We'll have merch available at all our shows, along with artist merch tables. Cash and card are typically accepted. Contact us for merch inquiries outside of show dates.

We're committed to making our shows accessible to everyone. Accessibility information, including wheelchair access, ADA-compliant restrooms, and accommodations, will be detailed on each event page. Contact us in advance for specific accessibility needs.
