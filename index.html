WHO WE ARE

LLP Events is Louisville's home for high-octane concert experiences. We don't just throw shows—we create sonic explosions that leave you breathless, sweaty, and hungry for more.

From the emotional intensity of emo to the aggressive power of nu-metal, we bring together communities of fans who live for the music, the energy, and the unforgettable moments that only live shows can deliver.
