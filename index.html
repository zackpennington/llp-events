FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Tickets to our shows are typically available both online and at the venue box office. Buying on the web will often mean extra dollars in ticket fees, which will increase the advertised price. If a show does not require a ticket, it should be noted in all publicity.

LLP Events believes that live music should be a family affair, so we do our best to keep the shows All Ages. This may not always be the case. Check individual show listings and contact us if you need to double-check for peace of mind.

LLP Events built their shows and fanbase at Headliners Music Hall in Louisville, KY, and will always consider it home. That said, shows may sometimes take place in other venues and in other cities. Check individual show listings for details.

All sales are final. If a show is cancelled, you should receive an automatic refund (online purchase) or be able to return your ticket at the point of purchase. If a show is postponed, existing tickets will be honored at a later date. Contact the venue if you aren't able to attend the rescheduled date.

Phones and point-and-click devices are allowed. If you want to bring a professional camera, please apply for a photo pass. Contact us here.

Parking will vary by venue. At Headliners, there is a pay lot next door and street parking on sections of Lexington and Payne Streets. Riding with a pal is always a good idea.

Yes! We love seeing you in our shirts and gear. You'll find merch tables for LLP Events and many of the individual projects of the bands and artists performing inside the venue. Tip your merch person, too. (And if you want merch AFTER the show, contact us and we will see what we have left.)

We strive to make all LLP Events as inclusive as possible. This means not just an accessible section of seating, but often ASL interpreters. Check the venue's website for arrival preferences and policies if you plan to use the accessible amenities. Contact us with any questions or concerns. And let us know if you have ideas on other ways we can make our shows more inclusive and welcoming.

We're always looking for talented musicians, vocalists, and crew members to join our shows. If you're interested in performing, helping backstage, or contributing in other ways, fill out our application form.
