THE LOUISVILLE LOVES CONCERT SERIES

LLP Events brings Louisville's most electrifying live music experiences to life. Each show in the Louisville Loves series is a genre-themed concert featuring a full live band and rotating cast of vocalists performing the songs you grew up screaming along to. From emo anthems and nu-metal breakdowns to grunge fury and arena rock, every show is a high-energy celebration of the music that shaped a generation.

Based at Headliners Music Hall on Lexington Road and Fourth Street Live! in downtown Louisville, our shows draw fans from across Kentucky, Indiana, Ohio, and beyond. Whether it's your first show or your tenth, the energy never gets old.

Headliners Music Hall

1386 Lexington Rd, Louisville, KY 40206

Louisville's premier live music venue on the Lexington Road corridor in the Highlands neighborhood. Headliners is the home stage of LLP Events, hosting our emo, nu-metal, grunge, and rock shows. The venue offers a standing-room main floor, balcony seating, and a full bar. All ages shows available.
